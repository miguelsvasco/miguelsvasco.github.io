About

Miguel S. Vasco is a PhD student in Computer Science from Técnico, University of Lisbon since 2018, and a researcher of the Group on Artificial Intelligence for People and Society (GAIPS) since 2016.


Miguel aims at merging the topics of Artificial Intelligence and Human Cognition with Deep Learning. His long term goal is to build artificial agents that learn as fast as humans do. He will certainly be succesfull in such task.


Robots, as most humans, perceive the world through multiple sensory channels which enable the aquisition of multimodal data from their environment. Miguel likes to construct human-inspired generative models for learning representations from multimodal data. 50% of the time, they work everytime.


Beyond working hard, Miguel likes to make people laugh, pretend he plays basketball well, compose music and write descriptions of himself in third-person.
